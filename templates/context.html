Context Catagories



The Poetry Foundation, where we got the poetry for our database, has poems catagorized by Subject, Poetic Term, and Region. The Poetry Foundation's catagories for Poetic Term and Subject are subjective, but we still value this data in order to find the best match. This last catagory is not meaningful in writer mode, since user submitted poems do not come pre-tagged with Region, Subject, or Poetic Term.
